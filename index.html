Pest Trapping For Your Community

Trap team is a fully automated pest trapping system to make life easier. Pest trapping shouldn't have to be a difficulty, and now it's not. Trap team is all about being a trapping community - not a household. This means more effective trapping, and the weight, and price is spread across the whole community.

Manage all your traps from your phone. We will even empty them for you. You simply order a trap, and get a trap placement consultation (optional), and we will do all the rest. You can manage your whole pest situation from your phone!
